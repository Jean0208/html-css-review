Welcome to Scoopalicious!

Serving the finest vegan ice cream in town, we specialize in crafting dairy-free delights that will send your taste buds into overdrive. From the moment you step inside our cozy urban shop, you'll know you're in for a treat!

Top 5 Fan Favourites

  • Choco-Obsession

    A rich, velvety dark chocolate ice cream made from a blend of organic cocoa and creamy coconut cream.

  • Very Berry Blast

    A harmonious explosion of raspberry, blackberry, and blueberry swirls in a smooth almond base.

  • Cookie Crumble Craze

    Scrummy cookie crumbles meet the creaminess of cashew-based vanilla ice cream.

  • Peanut Butter Paradise

    peanut butter ribbons swirled throughout a silky oat milk ice cream.

  • Mango Tango Sorbet

    A zesty dance of mango and lime, perfect for a refreshing treat on a hot summer day.
